DESCRIPTION

In the context of Indian cuisine, "masala" refers to a mixture of spices, either ground or in whole form, used to flavor dishes. It can be a dry spice blend, like Garam Masala, or a paste made from ingredients like ginger, garlic, onions, and tomatoes.
